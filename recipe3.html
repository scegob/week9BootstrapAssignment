Beef Samosas

Ingredients:

Yields dilling for 24 samosas

  • 1 lb (450 g) Ground beef
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) Xawaash
  • 2 tsp (10 mL) Vegeta Seasoning or 2 Maggi/Knorr bouillon cubes or 2 tsp salt
  • 2 Garlic (minced)
  • ¼ tsp (1.25 mL) Ground black pepper
  • 1 (350 g) Red onion (medium size)
  • ½ cup Cilantro
  • 24 sheets Samosa Pastry
  • Oil for frying

Directions:

Preparing the samosa filling

  1. Place the ground beef in a pan set on medium heat
  2. Add the xawaash seasoning
  3. Add the vegetable seasoning
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook the meat till no mois
  5. 1 Tbsp (15 mL) Baking powder
  6. 2 tsp (10 mL) Vanilla extract

Finishing the somosa filing

  1. Add the diced onion
  2. Add the washed and copped cilantro
  3. Mix well

Method of folding the samosa pastry

  1. Hold the pastry with the right angle towards you
  2. Fold one edge as shown
  3. Apply flour glue to the edge
  4. Lift the other corner
  5. Fold as shown
  6. Fill with the filling
  7. Tuck in the near side and apply flour glue
  8. Fold to get a perfect triangle
  9. Mix well

Frying the samosasg

  1. Fry the samosas in a medium heated oil
  2. Use a slotted spoon for safety
  3. Deep fry for 3 minutes
  4. Flip to the other side
  5. Fry for 2 more minutes
  6. Take them out with a slotted spoon
