Hello world, welcome to Simply Recipes the corner of the internet to showcase the wonders of Somali cuisine

The Federal Republic of Somalia, is a country in the Horn of Africa. The country is bordered by Ethiopia to the west, Djibouti to the northwest, the Gulf of Aden to the north, the Indian Ocean to the east, and Kenya to the southwest. Somalia has the longest coastline on Africa's mainland. Due to geographic and historical reasons, Somali cuisine has a wide mixture of diverse influences due to Somalia's rich tradition of trade and commerce.
